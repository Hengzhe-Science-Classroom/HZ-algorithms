# Algorithms · Interactive

**Design and analysis of algorithms**

**Status:** Planned

Part of [Hengzhe's Science Classroom](https://hengzhe-science-classroom.github.io/hub/) · Tier 4 · Graduate

## What You'll Learn

- Asymptotic analysis and recurrences
- Divide and conquer
- Dynamic programming
- Greedy algorithms
- Graph algorithms: BFS, DFS, shortest paths, MST
- Network flow
- NP-completeness and approximation

## References

- Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest & Clifford Stein, *Introduction to Algorithms* (4th ed., MIT Press)
- Jon Kleinberg & Éva Tardos, *Algorithm Design* (Pearson)
- Jeff Erickson, *Algorithms* (free textbook)

## Features (when built)

- **Interactive visualizations** — every concept comes alive with draggable, animated demos
- **KaTeX math rendering** — rigorous notation throughout
- **Exercises with hints & solutions** — from computational to proof-based
- **Progress tracking** — saved locally in your browser
- **Zero dependencies** — just open the link and learn
